## Notes

- `bun_dispatch` (`src/dispatch/lib.rs`) is a **proc-macro crate** — its 13 `unsafe`-keyword sites are inside `quote!` blocks and are *generated* into consumer crates, not executed by `bun_dispatch` itself. The runtime unsafe footprint of `bun_dispatch` is zero; the inventoried sites count toward whichever consumer crate instantiates `link_interface!` / `link_impl_*!`. Section L (`bun_install`), Section P (`bun_io`), and Section Q (`bun_http_jsc/headers_jsc.rs`, `bun_uws_sys/InternalLoopData.rs` etc.) all consume this macro.
- `bun_url_jsc` is empty for unsafe purposes — `lib.rs` only re-exports / extension-traits over `bun_url`. Phase-0 site count of 0 is correct.
- `bun_picohttp_sys` is a 2-decl FFI stub (`unsafe extern "C" {}` for `phr_*` symbols). The actual `phr_parse_request` consumer lives in `bun_picohttp`.
